Overview

My Project Idea which would help not only myself, but anyone with a similar career plan is a phone application

which would search for and find opponents of a similar skill ranking to compete against to help both the users to improve.

Regular match-making will pair you with teammates and opponents with a poor system making it extremely one sided for one of the teams, whereas this application

will let you decide whether you would like to do team versus team or a single player versus player, to make it so you can choose to either improve your own independance skills, or your ability to work together as a team.

Motivation

My motivation to make this application was that regular match-making doesn't seem as effective and or as efficient as I feel like it could be.

Personally, I feel as if my application idea for my custom match making for personal improvement will not only help me, but help better a lot of users.

A main issue with the regular match-making which League of Legends provides, is that there's no control over it and that leads to a mix of really

good and outstanding players being matched up with a few people who still have some things to work on, leading the better players to act aggressive

and hostile towards their under-performing teammates. With my applications ability to draft players, I feel as if this would solve a big ammount of these issues from occuring.

Description

Upon installing and creating an account on my Application, aswell as linking your account to the application allowing you to show your full profile to poentional teammates and or opponents,

the users would then have the ability to search through other players and be able to scout for teammates, opponents or even new friends to simply chat with.

Through the application, the users will have access to drafting teams against opposing teams and through the chatting system, will be able to pick a time which they would like to

work together, or verse eachother as a way for both of the players to improve. The application will also include a reporting feature to report abusive and unfair players.

Skills Required

The skills required to create my application would be high-end coding to avoid bugs and people who want to abuse the match-making by faking accounts.

The software and hardware shouldn't be too hard to aquire as a mediocre computer should be able to do the trick, though to find the skills may prove of some difficulty.

As a professional coder could be hired, if someone were to code their application by themselves without the advanced skills, they may struggle to avoid running into errors and it may take a decent amount of time to complete.

Outcome

If my project is successful, as it is expected to be, any player using the application are assumed to be improving at a faster rate as they would if they were just regularly playing the game.

Not only that, but with the better match-making system, the toxicity (toxicity refers to harrasment and abuse) of players is assumed to decrease to a lower level of which it is regularly at during normal match-making.
