What is Riot Games?

Riot Games is a company founded in 2006, with the intention to develop, publish and support the most player-focused games in the world.

In 2009, they released their initial debut, the most popular online multiplayer game known as League of Legends to a worldwide acclaim.

League of Legends didn't just explode Riot Games as a company, but blew up the entire E-Sports competetive scene as a whole.

Riot Games had used their insane popularity to try and increase their marketing by branching out from League of Legends, and had even

began to make music videos, cinematics, hologram live shows, a first person shooter, a auto-chess game, a card game, a phone version of League of Legends, and more.

They are even the creators of the extremely popular netflix show Arcane, which is based around characters from the game League of Legends, and their backstory growing up.

What is League of Legends?

League of Legends is a MOBA, which is a Multiplayer Online Battle Arena, set around a competetive scene. The game consists of two teams both having five players which all have the

same objective to complete, destroying the opponents Nexus, which would result in winning the game.

The game plays out with different players having a differnt way of playing which in all work around eachother and work together and over-time they build up strength and become stronger.
